Taiwan

I'm from Taiwan, an island in East Asia, features diverse geography with rugged mountains, fertile plains, and scenic coastlines. Neighboring countries include China to the west, Japan to the northeast, and the Philippines to the south. The Central Mountain Range, including Yu Shan, dominates the landscape, fostering rich biodiversity and vibrant cities.

Hualien

I was born in Hualien, located on Taiwan's east coast, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty. It boasts the breathtaking Taroko Gorge, lush mountains, and pristine beaches. Known for its vibrant indigenous culture and rich history, Hualien offers a serene escape with a mix of outdoor adventures and cultural experiences.
